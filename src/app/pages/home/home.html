Semantic Intelligence–Driven Clinical Submission Validation

At its core, Care2Data’s platform uses a governed Knowledge Graph architecture to shift validation from rule-based processes to explainable AI-driven reasoning.

1. Precision Validation

Eliminating Risk Before It Escalates

Our cross-domain reasoning capability, powered by linked data models, validates data contextually—not just structurally.

Instead of isolated rule checks, the platform understands relationships across domains (protocol, SDTM, ADaM, TLFs), detecting inconsistencies before they escalate into regulatory findings.

Result: Reduced submission risk. Increased regulatory confidence.

2. Clarity

Visualizing Impact. Accelerating Decisions

When protocols evolve, traditional systems create confusion. Care2Data highlights precisely which records are affected and why.

Clinical programmers gain immediate visibility into data impact, minimizing guesswork and accelerating resolution.

Result: Faster turnaround. Controlled change management.

3. Root Cause Intelligence

Beyond Error Flags — Toward True Insight

Most validation systems stop at error identification. Care2Data goes deeper.

Our Knowledge Core provides diagnostic explainable AI reasoning—explaining causal pathways behind discrepancies and integrity violations.

Result: Sustainable fixes instead of repetitive corrections.

4. Discovery & Impact Awareness

Cross-Domain Impact Reasoning

Semantic Intelligence enables proactive discovery of ripple effects across datasets and reports.

The platform not only identifies downstream impact but also recommends corrective pathways aligned with conformance and plausibility standards.

Result: Intelligent decision support across the entire clinical data lifecycle.
